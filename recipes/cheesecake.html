New York Cheesecake Recipe

Description

A creamy and rich New York-style cheesecake, perfect for any dessert lover.

Ingredients

  • 200g Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • 100g Unsalted Butter, melted
  • 900g Cream Cheese, softened
  • 200g Granulated Sugar
  • 200ml Sour Cream
  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 tbsp All-Purpose Flour

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 160°C (320°F).
  2. Mix the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter until combined, then press into the bottom of a springform pan to form the crust.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  4. Add the sour cream, eggs, vanilla extract, and flour, mixing until well combined.
  5. Pour the mixture over the crust in the springform pan.
  6. Bake for 60 minutes, then turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool inside with the door slightly open.
  7. Chill the cheesecake in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving.
