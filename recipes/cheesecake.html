Cheesecake Recipe

Description

A delicious yet basic NYC Cheesecake recipe for all the family.

Ingredients

  • Digestiive biscuits
  • Cheese
  • Vanilla
  • Lemon zest

Steps

  1. Bash up the digestive biscuits and crumble them into a cake tin.
  2. Compress the layer of biscuits until they form 1 hard consistent layer.
  3. Whisk up the zest, vanilla and cheese.
  4. Pour it on top, smooth it out and let it set in the fridge.
