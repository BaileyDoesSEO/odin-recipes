Sun Dried Tomato Pesto Pasta Recipe

Description

A delicious and simple pasta dish featuring the rich flavors of sun-dried tomato pesto.

Ingredients

  • 300g Penne Pasta
  • 1 Jar Sun-Dried Tomato Pesto
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 50g Grated Parmesan (optional)
  • Fresh Basil Leaves for garnish
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water according to the package instructions.
  2. Drain the pasta, reserving a small amount of pasta water.
  3. In a large pan, heat olive oil and stir in the sun-dried tomato pesto.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and mix well, adding reserved pasta water if needed to adjust consistency.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan and fresh basil leaves.
