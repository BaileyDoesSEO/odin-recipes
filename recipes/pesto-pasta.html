Sun Dried Pesto Pasta Recipe

Description

A delicious yet basic red pesto pasta recipe for all the family.

Ingredients

  • 1 Jar of Red Pesto
  • 300g Penne Pasta

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta for 11mins.
  2. Drain the pasta, reserving a small amount of water.
  3. Stir through the pesto sauce.
