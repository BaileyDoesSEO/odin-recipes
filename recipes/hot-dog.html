Times Square Hot Dog Recipe

Description

A classic New York-style hot dog inspired by the iconic street vendors of Times Square.

Ingredients

  • 4 Hot Dog Buns
  • 4 All-Beef Hot Dogs
  • 1 Cup Sauerkraut
  • 1/2 Cup Diced Onions
  • 1/4 Cup Yellow Mustard
  • 1/4 Cup Ketchup (optional)
  • Pickle Relish (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat the hot dogs by boiling, grilling, or steaming as per your preference.
  2. Warm the buns by lightly toasting them or steaming.
  3. Place a hot dog inside each bun.
  4. Top with sauerkraut, diced onions, mustard, ketchup, and relish as desired.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
